**AI Study Buddy** is an adaptive, AI-powered learning companion. Instead
of random quizzes, it learns your study style — where you're strong and
where you're weak — and adapts to help you learn more effectively. Built as
a Xiaomi MiMo submission showcasing AI automation in education.

## Stack

- Python
- Xiaomi MiMo (AI tutor + quiz generation)
- PDF / DOCX / TXT parsing (multi-language: Indonesian & English)

## Links

- Source: [github.com/g4rrzx/ai-study-buddy-agent](https://github.com/g4rrzx/ai-study-buddy-agent)

## Notes

The workflow starts with material upload — PDF, DOCX, TXT, or pasted text —
which the AI parses to extract content, identify key topics, and
auto-categorize by subject. From there it generates adaptive quizzes across
three difficulty levels, weighting more questions toward the topics you're
still weak on, with instant feedback and detailed explanations.

Around that core sit the features that make it feel like a real study
partner: a progress dashboard with per-topic mastery and weakness
detection, a context-aware AI tutor chat that remembers your uploaded
material, a Pomodoro focus mode, and smart reminders ahead of exams.
