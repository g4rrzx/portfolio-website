**IndoNews Summarizer** is an AI-powered news aggregator for Indonesian
media. It pulls articles from trusted outlets and automatically condenses
them into five concise bullet points — cutting reading time from around 30
minutes down to 5.

## Stack

- Python
- NLP / AI summarization
- RSS feeds + web scraping

## Links

- Source: [github.com/g4rrzx/indonews-summarizer](https://github.com/g4rrzx/indonews-summarizer)

## Notes

The agent aggregates from major Indonesian sources — Detik, Kompas, Tempo,
Antara, and Tech in Asia ID — refreshing roughly every 15 minutes. Each
1000+ word article is distilled into five bullet points that preserve
context and nuance while stripping the filler, in natural Bahasa
Indonesia.

It also does smart topic filtering across Tech, Business, Crypto,
Politics, Sports, and Entertainment, plus a daily digest that delivers the
top stories of the day as a five-minute read. The goal is a practical NLP
use case: stay informed without drowning in long-form articles.
